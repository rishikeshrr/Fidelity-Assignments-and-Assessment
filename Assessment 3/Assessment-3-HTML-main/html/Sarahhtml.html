About

X-Men is an American superhero film series based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. The series was produced by 20th Century Fox and Marvel Entertainment from 2000 to 2020. Fox obtained the film rights to the team and other related characters in 1994 for $2.6 million.

Directors: James Mangold, Gavin Hood, Brett Ratner, Matthew Vaughn, Simon Kinberg, Josh Boone, Bryan Singer
